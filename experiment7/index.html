Experiment 7 - Data Visualization & Networks

Description

This is a chart that visualizes our moods in a given day. It's inspired by the shape of an aurora. The X-axis charts the time while the y-axis is the intensity of the emotion. The colors represent different moods. You guess what a color correlates to a color.

Technical

We used the chart.js library and parsed through a csv file that contained our charted moods within the day.

Reflection

We used a floating bar graph, because we wanted to emphasize the range in emotion and mood shifts throughout the day. This would also better mimic the shape of an aurora. In the end, I believe we could innovate further, but with the given time constraints and other repsonsiblities, this is our final product.
